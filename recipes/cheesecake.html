Cheesecake

A simple and delicious cheesecake recipe.

Ingredients

For the crust

  • 85g butter
  • 140g digestive biscuits
  • 1 tbsp butter

For the filling

  • 900g philadelphia cheese
  • 250g golden caster sugar
  • 3 tbsp plain flower
  • 1.5 tsp vanilla extract
  • zest of 1 lemon
  • 1.5 tsp lemon juice
  • 3 large eggs + 1 yolk
  • 284ml carton soured cream

For the topping

  • 142ml carton soured cream
  • 1 tbsp golden caster sugar
  • 2 tsp lemon juice
